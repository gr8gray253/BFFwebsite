Data Deletion Request

If you have used Facebook or Google to log into the Bayou Family Fishing & Charity Member Portal and would like your data removed from our systems, you can request deletion at any time. We will process your request within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

Upon receiving a deletion request, we will permanently remove:

  • Your member account and profile information (name, email, profile photo)
  • All fishing pins and photos you have posted to the member map
  • All comments you have posted
  • Any other data associated with your account
Note: Deleting your BFF account does not delete your Google or Facebook account. To manage or delete those accounts, visit myaccount.google.com or Facebook Settings.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to kyle@bayoucharity.org with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include the name and email address associated with your BFF account so we can locate your data
  3. We will confirm receipt within 5 business days and complete deletion within 30 days
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been fully removed
