Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Bayou Family Fishing & Charity ("BFF," "we," "our," or "us") operates the website bayoucharity.org and the BFF Member Portal. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our members and visitors. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you create a BFF member account using Google or Facebook login, we receive basic profile information from those platforms, which may include:

  • Your name or display name
  • Your email address
  • Your profile photo

When you use the BFF Member Map, we may also collect:

  • Photos you choose to upload
  • Location data you provide when placing a pin on the map (latitude and longitude)
  • Captions, fish species, and other information you enter voluntarily
  • Comments you post on other members' pins

We do not collect payment information, Social Security numbers, or sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your BFF member account
  • Display your fishing pins and posts on the member map and community feed
  • Notify our administrator of new signups for account approval
  • Communicate with you about your membership or BFF events
  • Improve the website and member experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. How We Store Your Information

Member account data and posted content are stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Photos you upload are stored in a secure cloud storage bucket. We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls.

4. Third-Party Login Providers

We offer sign-in through Google and Facebook as a convenience. When you use these services to log in, you are also subject to their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Privacy Policy

BFF only receives the basic profile information described in Section 1. We do not receive your passwords or payment information from these platforms.

5. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged into your member account. We do not use advertising cookies or track your activity across other websites.

6. Children's Privacy

The BFF Member Portal is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

To exercise any of these rights, please see our Data Deletion page or contact us directly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
